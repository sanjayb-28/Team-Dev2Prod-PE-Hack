# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Dev2Prod is a technical project, but collaboration still matters. We expect contributors to be respectful, direct, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- focus on the work, not the person
- give technical feedback clearly and without hostility
- assume good intent while still holding a high engineering bar
- keep discussions constructive and relevant to the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other repository interactions.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions that do not meet this standard.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the repository maintainers through GitHub.
